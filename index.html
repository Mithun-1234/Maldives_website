About Us

Your Trusted Partner in India

Kashika Travels is a dedicated service provider assisting Maldivian nationals in India for medical and travel-related needs. From doctor appointments and hospital guidance to travel bookings and SIM card activation, we are here to make your journey smooth, safe, and stress-free.

What began as a simple effort to support Maldivian patients in Indian hospitals has now grown into a complete medical and travel assistance service. We’ve helped hundreds of individuals and families feel at home while navigating complex medical systems.

With deep knowledge of hospitals, cities, and cultural needs, our team ensures every step of your visit is clear and comfortable.

Our mission is to simplify the experience of Maldivians in India by offering reliable, affordable, and respectful services that cover all their travel and healthcare needs.

From translation and appointments to travel and accommodations — we’re here to help every step of the way.

We envision a future where every Maldivian visiting India feels supported, respected, and fully taken care of through our services.

Our goal is to be the most trusted name in cross-border patient care and travel support between Maldives and India.
